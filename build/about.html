Informed Consent
Please read the information carefully. You must be at least 18 years of age to participate in this study.

Purpose of the research
The aim of the study is to examine the hierarchical representation of different themes in chess by segmenting events. It is conducted as part of scientific research carried out by the Department of Cognitive Science and Psychology. The study has received approval from the Committee for Research Ethics, Department of Cognitive Science and Psychology, New Bulgarian University.

Procedure
You will be shown a series of chess game sequences on this website. The task is to press the red button on your screen, when you believe one meaningful chess sequence ends and another begins.

Duration
15 Minutes

Voluntary Participation
Your participation is on an entirely voluntary basis, it comes with no known risks and without any form of compensation. You are free to withdraw your participation at any time and without giving a reason. Withdrawing from this study has absolutely no consequences.

Confidentiality
You will be asked to provide your chess ranking, gender and age. To ensure data security, Google's Firebase Services will be utilized, which offers encrypted storage and access controls to prevent unauthorized access and data breaches. Your anonymity will be guaranteed throughout the data processing and reporting stages by utilizing unique identifiers instead of personal information.

Contact information for questions or concerns
If you have any questions about the upcoming procedure, please direct them to Patric Pfoertner M.Sc., PhD Candidate (f85787@students.nbu.bg or p.pfoertner@psychology4.me)
